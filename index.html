CLAIRO

Claire Contrill, known by her stage name Clairo, is a 21-year-old singer-songwriter from Boston. A “bedroom pop” artist, she rose to fame after releasing “Pretty Girl” — a music video shot on PhotoBooth in her room — in 2017. Her eclectic lo-fi debut album, Immunity, was released in August 2019. The album, produced by Clairo and Rostam Batmanglij, is both intimate and wispy. The 11 tracks tell stories about love and desire, quiet moments in the artist’s first relationships and the pain in navigating dependability.

In an interview with Beats 1, Clairo said, "People like that I can relate to them. That's my number one thing that I want to maintain, that I want to be a friend to the people who are listening to the music and I want them to feel comfortable." That same month, she was named Apple Music’s ‘Up Next’ artist. She has over 5 million monthly listeners on Spotify and is currently headlining a North American tour.
